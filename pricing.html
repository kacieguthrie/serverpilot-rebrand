Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a trial period?

We have a 14 day trial period that begins when you sign up. No credit card is required. The trial period lets you test basic functionality to see how ServerPilot works.

How do I end my trial and begin using paid features?

As soon as you add your credit card or PayPal credit, your trial automatically ends.

Do you accept PayPal?

Yes, we do

When will my card be charged?

Your card will be charged after each month has ended. For example, your card will be charged for July's usage on August 1st.

Can I have servers on separate plans?

Yes, each server can be on a different plan.

Can I change my server's plan?

You can upgrade or downgrade a server at any time.

What if I connect or delete a server partway through the month?

That server and the apps on it will be billed hourly for the time on each plan.

What if I decide to cancel my service?

Once you delete your servers, usage fees stop accruing. Your final invoice for the period of the month before you deleted your servers will be charged after the month is over.
